At risk of being flooded?
CHECK FOR EMERGENCY ALERTS AND WEATHER WARNINGS BEFOREHAND!
Before a flood
-Plan an escape route.
-Close all windows and doors.
-Move important items to a high ground.
-Move vechicles to a higher ground.

During the flood.
-Do not try to swim, walk or drive through flooded waters, as you can easily be swept away.
-Do not walk on sea defences or river banks.
-Avoid any contact with flood water, as it may pose the risk of contamination.


After the flood
-Boil tap water/drink bottled water.
-Dispose of any food that may be contaminated with the flood water.
-Take pictures of the damage, this could help with hoime insurance claims.
